# UI Design Principles

- Establish visual hierarchy with restrained scale, weight, spacing, and contrast.
- Use layout rhythm: consistent grid gaps, predictable alignment, and stable component dimensions.
- Design mobile-first with touch targets, readable text, and non-overlapping panels.
- Keep dashboards dense but calm: summarize status, expose actions, and avoid marketing clutter.
- Make forms explicit with labels, validation states, and recovery paths.
- Treat empty, loading, and error states as primary product states.
- Use accessible contrast and avoid relying on color alone.
- Prefer clear typography, short labels, and consistent button/icon semantics.
- Dark UI needs enough surface contrast and careful accent use.
- Premium SaaS styling comes from restraint, spacing, polish, and useful density.
